SAT Tutoring

Reading, Writing, and Math part-time SAT Tutor at Varsity Tutors.

My Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is centered around the student themselves instead of the content. What I mean by that is that I prioritize getting an understanding of the students learning style, their strengths, and areas that they need to working on.

Every student is different, which is why sometimes standardized teaching doesn't work for some. Knowing a student allows me to optimize their learning experience for them to make the most out of the session but most importantly remember what they learned and be able to apply the skills during an exam.

What I've Learned

Being a tutor may involve teaching but I have found that I have personally learned a lot of things through this experience. Tutoring has definitely nourished my communication skills. Having to convey the solution to an advanced math problem, or the meaning behind a piece of classic literature is not easy and it takes skill to communicate these in a understandable way.

Tutoring has also made me a better leader. Guiding students to achieve their desired scores takes work. Simply explaining solutions to students often does not work and in order for them to learn I had to learn to take initiative during sessions and be able to improvise and adapt on the spot for the students needs.

Future Service Plans

In the future I look forward to being more involved with my community by participating in more volunteering activities. I think that getting involved can be a win-win scenario for both me and my community.
